About


Humans can easily recognize bodies and sign language. This is made possible by a combination of visual and synaptic interactions that occurred during brain development. Reproducing this ability on a computer requires solving many questions, such as how to separate objects in images and which image capture and classification techniques are more appropriate.

In this gesture-based desktop automation project, the model is first trained with images of various hand gestures. Point to the numbers with your fingers like 1, 2, 3, 4. This model uses the built-in webcam to capture video images. Images of gestures captured in video frames are compared with pre-trained models to identify gestures.
